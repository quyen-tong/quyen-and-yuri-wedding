Things to Do

outdoorsy

Multnomah Falls *
30 minute drive from Portland. Gorgeous views and easy trek to main lookout (Lower Multnomah Falls). Or do the full hike if you're feeling adventurous.

Peninsula Park and Rose Gardens
Park with dozens of variety of B.E.A.utiful roses.

Cannon Beach
Quaint beach town 75 minutes by car from Portland. We like to do bonfires during the summer.

Sites

The Grotto - National Sanctuary of Our Sorrowful Mother *
Catholic outdoor shrine with botanical gardens and chapels.

Portland Japanese Garden
Serene Japanese garden with beautiful teahouses and view of Mt. Hood.

Shopping

Oregon has no sales tax! Great opportunity to save money on high-priced items 🛍️

Downtown Portland
Pioneer Courhouse Square is a good starting point if you want to do pedestrian shopping. Stores like Nordstrom, Gucci, & Apple are within a one mile radius. MUJI is one of Quyen's favorite spots.

Vintage / Thrifting
Portland has a really good vintage scene. If you are into thrifting, we definitely recommend checking out a couple of shops.

Eats

Cartopia
Group of food carts. If you want to feel like a Portlander, grab grub at one of the hundreds of food carts.

Prost!
Our favorite bar. Serves German beers and snacks in a bier garten setting.

Voodoo Donuts
Best damn donut.

Coffee Shops
Lots of quality coffee shops sprinkled around town, especially downtown.

Q & A

How far from Portland is Vancouver, WA?
Portand and Vancouver are neighboring cities divided by the Columbia River which also separates Oregon and Washington.




How good is the public transit?
Portland has a decent train system. If you're staying in Portland proper, you can get by on public transportation.


Are children welcome?
Our wedding is an adults-only event. We appreciate you making arrangements ahead of time so you can celebrate with us.


Can I bring a plus-one?
Our wedding is strictly RSVP. Certain guests are granted a plus-one which will be addressed on invitation.


What is the dress code?
Semi-formal or cocktail attire.


Where should guests park at the venue? Is parking free?
The venue provides parking at no cost.


Where are the ceremony and reception taking place?
Both ceremony and reception will be at Moonlight Events.


Will the ceremony and reception be indoors or outdoors?
Both ceremony reception will be indoors. However, there is a cute outdoor courtyard in the back where you can congregate (weather permitting).


Will there be an open bar?
Yes, an open bar will be provided. Be sure to tip our lovely bartender(s).


What kind of food and drink will be served?
We will be serving lemon peppered chicken with a creamy lemon sauce and roasted seasonal vegetables. Those who indicated dietary restriction will be served alfredo pasta with roasted seasonal vegetables.


What if I can't make it to the wedding?
Please let us know by selecting "No" on the RSVP form so we can plan accordingly.
